## Key Framing
The paper should begin with enterprise understanding, not EKOS internals.

Preferred framing:

> Enterprise AI needs durable enterprise meaning.

Rejected framing:

- Enterprise AI needs ontology.
- Enterprise AI needs GraphRAG.
- Enterprise AI needs better agents.

Those are implementation paths. The root problem is that enterprise meaning is implicit, fragmented, and hard for AI systems to use reliably.
