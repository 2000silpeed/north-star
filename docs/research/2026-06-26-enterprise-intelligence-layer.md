# Research Note — Enterprise Intelligence Layer

Date: 2026-06-26

## Core Insight
AI models evolve continuously.
Enterprise knowledge evolves slowly.
Therefore, the stable architectural target should not be a specific AI model but an Enterprise Intelligence Layer.

## Working Thesis
EKOS should become a durable semantic foundation that allows any current or future AI model to understand enterprise systems consistently.

## Important Conclusions
- AI is replaceable.
- Enterprise semantics are long-lived.
- Enterprise structure should be separated from AI implementation.
- AI becomes a consumer of enterprise intelligence rather than the owner of enterprise knowledge.

## Design Implications
- Ontology is an implementation mechanism, not the product.
- Knowledge Graph is an implementation detail, not the message.
- Enterprise Intelligence Layer is the architectural concept presented externally.

## Open Questions
- Which enterprise concepts are universal across ERP, CRM, MES and PLM?
- How should enterprise semantics evolve without breaking AI compatibility?
- How much human review is required before semantic changes become canonical?

Status: Working hypothesis. Validate through architecture, prototypes and future case studies.
